## Sync Configuration Options

### Daily Conversation Summary

When enabled, CurrentClient automatically creates a comprehensive summary of all client interactions from the previous day and adds them to Quivr activity records each morning.

**What's included:**
- Text message conversations with full context
- Phone call details (duration, time, participants)
- Key topics discussed
- Action items identified

**Customization options:**
- Include both calls and texts (default)
- Text-only mode (exclude phone calls)
- Custom summary formatting

### Real-time Sync

For immediate data availability, enable real-time sync to push communications to Quivr as they happen.

**Benefits:**
- Instant visibility in Quivr dashboards
- No delay in compliance tracking
- Real-time team collaboration
- Immediate reporting capabilities

## Conversation Notes Sync

Your CurrentClient conversations automatically become organized notes in Quivr, providing a comprehensive communication history for each client.

<Note>
  **Conversation summaries:** CurrentClient automatically creates detailed conversation summaries that are added as activity records to your Quivr client profiles, ensuring complete documentation of all client interactions.
</Note>

## Best Practices

- **Review sync settings regularly** to ensure they match your workflow
- **Use tags consistently** between both systems for better organization
- **Enable real-time sync** for time-sensitive client communications
- **Monitor daily summaries** to ensure accurate activity tracking

## Troubleshooting Sync Issues

If data isn't syncing as expected:

1. Verify your Quivr connection is active in Settings
2. Check that sync options are properly configured
3. Ensure you have proper permissions in both systems
4. Contact support if issues persist
