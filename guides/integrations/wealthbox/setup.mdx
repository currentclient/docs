## Setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to integrations in CurrentClient">
    Navigate to [Settings â†’ Integrations](https://app.currentclient.com/app/settings/crm-sync) in your CurrentClient account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find Wealthbox">
    Look for Wealthbox in the list of available integrations and click **Connect**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize the connection">
    You'll be redirected to Wealthbox to log in and authorize CurrentClient access
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure initial settings">
    Choose your sync preferences and data mapping options
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test the connection">
    We'll run a quick test to make sure everything is working properly
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**First sync timing:** After connecting, your initial data sync can take 15-30 minutes depending on how many contacts you have in Wealthbox.
</Note>

## What happens next?

Once setup is complete:
- Your Wealthbox contacts will start appearing in CurrentClient
- Communications will begin logging to client records

Having trouble with setup? Check our [troubleshooting guide](/guides/integrations/wealthbox/troubleshooting) or reach out for help!
