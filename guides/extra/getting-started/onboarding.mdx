## Onboarding Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Business Information">
    Enter your company name, industry, and business type
  </Step>
  <Step title="User Profile">
    Add your name, title, and profile photo
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phone Setup">
    Configure your business phone number and calling preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notification Settings">
    Set up email notifications and alert preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import Contacts">
    Upload your existing contact list or add initial contacts
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dashboard Tour">
    Get familiar with the main navigation and features
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Setup Questions

<Accordion title="What happens if I skip onboarding?">
  You can always return to complete onboarding later, but some features may be limited until key steps are completed, particularly phone registration and business verification.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change settings later?">
  Yes, all onboarding settings can be modified later in your account settings. However, some changes like business verification may require additional steps.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need to add contacts during onboarding?">
  While not required, adding at least a few test contacts during onboarding helps you understand the system better and test features immediately.
</Accordion>
