## Create Your CurrentClient Account

Get started with CurrentClient by creating your new account. This quick tutorial walks you through the sign-up process and initial account setup.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page">
    Navigate to the CurrentClient registration page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Information">
    Provide your business email, name, and basic company details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your email and click the verification link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Password">
    Create a strong password for your account
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Make sure you have your business email and phone number ready before starting
  the registration process.
</Info>

## What You'll Need

Before creating your account, gather these items:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Business Email" icon="envelope">
    A professional email address for your account
  </Card>
  <Card title="Company Information" icon="building">
    Your business name and basic company details
  </Card>
  <Card title="Phone Number" icon="phone">
    A business phone number for verification
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payment Method" icon="credit-card">
    Credit card or payment information for your subscription
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  **Pro Tip:** Use your main business email address as this will be your primary
  login and will receive all system notifications.
</Tip>
