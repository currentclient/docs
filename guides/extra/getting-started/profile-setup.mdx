## Profile Best Practices

<Accordion title="Profile Photo Guidelines">
  - Use a professional headshot with good lighting
  - Ensure the photo is clear and recent
  - Crop to focus on your face and shoulders  
  - Use a neutral or professional background
  - Keep the file size under 2MB for faster loading
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Business Information Tips">
  - Be specific about your industry and services
  - Include relevant certifications or specializations
  - Keep descriptions concise but informative
  - Update information as your business evolves
</Accordion>

## Privacy & Security Settings

<Info>
  **Privacy Control:** You can control what information is visible to contacts and what remains private to your team. Review privacy settings during profile setup.
</Info>

<Warning>
  **Security Reminder:** Never include sensitive information like social security numbers or personal addresses in your public profile.
</Warning>

## Profile Impact on Client Experience

A complete profile enhances your client experience by:

- **Building Trust** - Professional photo and details create credibility
- **Personal Connection** - Clients feel more comfortable with a real person
- **Brand Consistency** - Consistent information across all communications  
- **Professional Image** - Complete profiles convey attention to detail
