## Notification Categories

### Campaign Notifications
- **Broadcast Sent:** Confirmation when campaigns are delivered
- **Response Received:** Alert when contacts reply to broadcasts
- **Campaign Completed:** Summary when broadcast delivery is finished
- **Delivery Failures:** Notification of failed message deliveries

### Account Activity
- **New Contact Added:** Alerts for new contact additions
- **Contact Updates:** Notifications when contact information changes
- **Integration Sync:** Updates on CRM synchronization status
- **System Maintenance:** Advance notice of scheduled maintenance

### Performance Alerts
- **Low Delivery Rates:** Warning when delivery rates drop below threshold
- **High Opt-out Rates:** Alert when unsubscribe rates increase
- **Engagement Milestones:** Notifications for campaign performance goals
- **Quota Alerts:** Warnings when approaching message limits

<Accordion title="Notification Frequency Options">
  **Real-time (Immediate):**
  - Best for urgent responses and critical alerts
  - May result in many emails during busy periods
  - Ideal for small teams or high-priority contacts

  **Hourly Batches:**
  - Groups notifications into hourly summaries
  - Reduces email volume while maintaining responsiveness
  - Good balance for most business needs

  **Daily Summaries:**
  - Single daily report with all activity
  - Minimal email volume but less immediate
  - Best for overview and trend monitoring
</Accordion>

## Notification Best Practices

<Warning>
  **Avoid Notification Fatigue:** Too many notifications can lead to important alerts being ignored. Be selective about what triggers notifications.
</Warning>

### Optimization Tips

- **Start Conservative:** Begin with fewer notifications and add more as needed
- **Regular Review:** Monthly review of notification effectiveness and volume
- **Team Feedback:** Get input from team members on notification usefulness
- **Performance Impact:** Monitor if notifications improve response times and outcomes

### Managing Volume

- Use daily summaries for routine activity
- Reserve real-time alerts for urgent matters only
- Group related notifications when possible
- Set quiet hours for non-critical notifications

<Tip>
  **Success Strategy:** The most effective notification setup balances staying informed with avoiding information overload. Customize based on your team's workflow and priorities.
</Tip>
