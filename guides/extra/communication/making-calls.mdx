## Call Quality Best Practices

<Accordion title="Equipment Recommendations">
  - **Headset:** Use a professional headset for clear audio - **Internet:**
  Ensure stable high-speed internet connection - **Environment:** Find a quiet
  space with minimal background noise - **Backup:** Have a backup calling method
  ready (mobile phone)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Professional Call Etiquette">
  - Start with a clear introduction and purpose - Speak clearly and at an
  appropriate pace - Listen actively and take notes during the call - Summarize
  next steps before ending the call - Follow up with written confirmation if
  needed
</Accordion>

## Call Controls

During active calls, you have access to:

- **Mute/Unmute** - Control your microphone
- **Hold** - Place the call on hold temporarily
- **Transfer** - Transfer calls to team members
- **Record** - Start/stop call recording
- **Keypad** - Access dial tones for phone menus
- **Notes** - Add notes without ending the call

<Info>
  **Call Recording:** Check local laws regarding call recording requirements.
  Some jurisdictions require two-party consent before recording calls.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting Call Issues

<Accordion title="Audio Quality Problems">
  **Common Solutions:** - Check internet connection speed and stability - Close
  other applications using bandwidth - Use a wired internet connection instead
  of Wi-Fi - Restart your browser or the CurrentClient app - Check microphone
  and speaker settings
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Connection Issues">
  **Troubleshooting Steps:** - Verify your phone registration is complete -
  Check if the recipient's number is correct - Try calling a different number to
  test your setup - Contact support if issues persist across multiple calls
</Accordion>

<Warning>
  **Call Quality:** Poor call quality can negatively impact client
  relationships. Always test your setup before important calls and have a backup
  plan ready.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  **Pro Tip:** Use CurrentClient's call scheduling feature to set up calls in
  advance, ensuring both you and your contacts are prepared and available.
</Tip>
