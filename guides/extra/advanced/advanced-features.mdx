</Accordion>

## Conversation Management

<iframe
  src="https://www.loom.com/embed/cf9ea882d9224c34b148634d2ff70655"
  width="100%"
  height="400"
  frameBorder={0}
  allowFullScreen
/>

**Duration:** 2 minutes

Understand the difference between active and completed conversations and how to effectively manage your communication workflow.

<Accordion title="Active vs Completed Conversations">
  **Active Conversations:** - Ongoing client discussions requiring follow-up -
  New messages or recent activity - Pending responses or actions needed
  **Completed Conversations:** - Resolved client issues or completed
  transactions - Archived for record-keeping - No immediate action required
</Accordion>

<Tip>
  **Workflow Tip:** Regularly review and categorize conversations to maintain an
  organized inbox and ensure no client communications fall through the cracks.
</Tip>
