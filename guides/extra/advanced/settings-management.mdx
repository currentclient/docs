## Conversation Workflow Management

<Accordion title="Active vs Completed Conversations">
  **Active Conversations:**
  - Ongoing client discussions requiring follow-up
  - New messages or recent activity
  - Pending responses or actions needed
  - High-priority client communications
  
  **Completed Conversations:**  
  - Resolved client issues or completed transactions
  - Archived for record-keeping
  - No immediate action required
  - Reference material for future interactions
</Accordion>

## Advanced Conversation Features

### Conversation Categorization
- **Priority Levels:** Mark conversations as urgent, normal, or low priority
- **Topic Tags:** Categorize conversations by subject matter
- **Follow-up Reminders:** Set automatic reminders for pending actions
- **Team Assignment:** Assign conversations to specific team members

### Workflow Automation
- **Auto-Archiving:** Automatically move old conversations to completed status
- **Response Templates:** Create quick responses for common scenarios
- **Escalation Rules:** Automatically escalate unresponded conversations
- **Integration Triggers:** Sync conversation status with CRM systems

<Info>
  **Workflow Tip:** Regularly review and categorize conversations to maintain an organized inbox and ensure no client communications fall through the cracks.
</Info>

## Advanced Notification Configuration

### Notification Rules
- **Time-Based Rules:** Different notifications for business vs. after hours
- **Priority Filtering:** Only get alerts for high-priority contacts
- **Channel Preferences:** Set different notifications for calls vs. messages
- **Team Notifications:** Configure team-wide alert distribution

### Custom Alert Conditions
- **VIP Contacts:** Special alerts for your most important clients
- **Keywords:** Get notified when specific words appear in messages
- **Missed Interactions:** Alerts for unanswered calls or messages
- **Performance Thresholds:** Notifications when metrics exceed limits

## Security and Access Control

### Account Security Features
- **Two-Factor Authentication:** Add extra security to your account
- **Access Logging:** Monitor login activity and suspicious behavior
- **IP Restrictions:** Limit access to specific IP addresses or ranges
- **Session Management:** Control active session limits and timeouts

### Team Access Management
- **Role-Based Permissions:** Define what each team member can access
- **Feature Restrictions:** Limit access to sensitive features or data
- **Audit Trails:** Track all user actions for compliance purposes
- **Temporary Access:** Grant limited-time access for contractors or consultants

<Warning>
  **Security Best Practices:** Regularly review access permissions and security settings, especially when team members change roles or leave your organization.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting Advanced Settings

<Accordion title="Common Configuration Issues">
  **Settings Not Saving:**
  - Clear browser cache and cookies
  - Check browser compatibility requirements
  - Verify account permissions for configuration changes
  - Contact support if problems persist

  **Team Settings Conflicts:**
  - Review role-based permission overlaps
  - Check for conflicting notification rules
  - Verify team member access levels
  - Coordinate settings changes with administrators
</Accordion>
