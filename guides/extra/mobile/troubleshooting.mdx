## Fixing Notification Issues

<Accordion title="iOS Notification Setup">
  **Check these iOS settings:**
  1. Settings > Notifications > CurrentClient
  2. Ensure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
  3. Turn on "Badge App Icon" and "Sounds"
  4. Set "Show Previews" to "Always" or "When Unlocked"
  5. Verify "Critical Alerts" is enabled if available
  6. Check that Focus/Do Not Disturb isn't blocking CurrentClient
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Android Notification Setup">
  **Check these Android settings:**
  1. Settings > Apps > CurrentClient > Notifications
  2. Enable "Show notifications"
  3. Turn on all notification categories
  4. Settings > Battery > Battery Optimization
  5. Set CurrentClient to "Don't optimize"
  6. Check Do Not Disturb exceptions include CurrentClient
</Accordion>

## Battery Optimization Settings

<Warning>
  **Critical Setting:** Disable battery optimization for CurrentClient to ensure you receive calls even when your phone is idle or in sleep mode.
</Warning>

### iOS Battery Settings
- Settings > Battery > Background App Refresh
- Enable Background App Refresh for CurrentClient
- Settings > Screen Time > App Limits (ensure CurrentClient isn't limited)

### Android Battery Settings
- Settings > Battery > Battery Optimization
- Find CurrentClient and select "Don't optimize"
- Settings > Apps > CurrentClient > Battery
- Set to "No restrictions" or "Allow background activity"

## Advanced Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="App Crashes or Freezing">
  **Troubleshooting Steps:**
  1. Force close and restart the CurrentClient app
  2. Check available device storage (need 1GB+ free)
  3. Clear app cache (Android) or offload app (iOS)
  4. Update to the latest app version
  5. Restart your device completely
  6. Uninstall and reinstall CurrentClient if issues persist
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Contact Sync Issues">
  **Resolution Steps:**
  1. Check internet connection stability
  2. Verify account login status in the app
  3. Force sync by pulling down on contact list
  4. Log out and log back into the app
  5. Check if desktop and mobile contacts match
  6. Contact support if sync continues to fail
</Accordion>

## When to Contact Support

Contact CurrentClient support if you experience:
- Persistent call quality issues after troubleshooting
- Inability to receive calls despite proper setup
- App crashes that continue after reinstalling
- Contact sync failures that don't resolve with standard steps
- Billing or account access issues

<Info>
  **Support Resources:** Include your device model, operating system version, and app version when contacting support for faster resolution.
</Info>

<Tip>
  **Preventive Maintenance:** Regularly update the CurrentClient app and your device's operating system to prevent many common issues before they occur.
</Tip>
