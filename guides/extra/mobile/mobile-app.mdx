## Troubleshoot Ringing Issues

<iframe 
  src="https://www.loom.com/embed/70c39ff757314f3e83b00125fabbfbf1" 
  width="100%" 
  height="400"
  frameBorder={0} 
  allowFullScreen
/>

**Duration:** 2 minutes

Resolve common issues with call notifications and ringing on the mobile app to ensure you never miss important client calls.

<Accordion title="Common Ringing Issues">
  - **App Permissions:** Ensure CurrentClient has permission to access phone and notifications
  - **Background App Refresh:** Enable background refresh for the CurrentClient app
  - **Do Not Disturb:** Check that DND settings aren't blocking CurrentClient notifications
  - **Battery Optimization:** Disable battery optimization for CurrentClient to keep it running
</Accordion>

<Warning>
  **Important:** Make sure your mobile device allows CurrentClient to run in the background and send notifications for reliable call reception.
</Warning>
