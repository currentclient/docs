# Port from Google Voice Business

<Warning>
  Requires Google Workspace admin access. Contact your IT administrator if
  needed.
</Warning>

## What you'll need from Google Admin

All information comes from your Google Admin console:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Account ID" icon="credit-card">
    16-digit billing account ID (1234-5678-9012-3456)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transfer PIN" icon="key">
    Port-out PIN from Number porting settings
  </Card>
  <Card title="Billing Address" icon="map-pin">
    Address from Google Voice Locations
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Step-by-step process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Account ID">
    - Google Admin → **Billing → Payment Accounts → Account ID**
    - Choose the account linked to Google Voice (16 digits)
  </Step>

<Step title="Get Transfer PIN">
  - Google Admin → **Apps → Google Workspace → Google Voice → Number porting →
  Port-out info** - Click **Preview** if needed to see the PIN
</Step>

<Step title="Get Billing Address">
  - Google Admin → **Apps → Google Workspace → Google Voice → Locations** - Use
  the address associated with your Google Voice service
</Step>

  <Step title="Submit port request">
    - Visit [Port a number](https://app.currentclient.com/app/settings/port-in)
    - Select **Port Type: Calls & Texts**
    - Enter the information from steps 1-3
    - Submit your request
  </Step>
</Steps>
