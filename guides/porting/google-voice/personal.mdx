# Port from Google Voice Personal

<Tip>
  Google charges a small fee to unlock numbers for porting. This is paid to
  Google, not CurrentClient.
</Tip>

## What you'll need from Google Voice

Before starting the CurrentClient port request, gather this information:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Account Number" icon="phone">
    Your Google Voice phone number
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transfer PIN" icon="key">
    PIN from Google Voice voicemail settings
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Step-by-step process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Unlock your number in Google Voice">
    - Go to **Settings → Account → Unlock** in Google Voice
    - Follow prompts and complete Google's payment
  </Step>

<Step title="Get your Transfer PIN">
  - In Google Voice, go to **Settings → Voicemail** - Enable "Call to listen"
  and set a PIN - This PIN is your **Transfer PIN**
</Step>

<Step title="Start port request in CurrentClient">
  - Visit [Port a number](https://app.currentclient.com/app/settings/port-in) -
  Select **Port Type: Calls & Texts** - Enter your Google Voice number as the
  **Account Number** - Enter the PIN from step 2 as the **Transfer PIN**
</Step>

  <Step title="Upload proof of account">
    - Screenshot your Google Voice settings page
    - Make sure your phone number is visible
    - Upload as **Billing Statement**
  </Step>
</Steps>
