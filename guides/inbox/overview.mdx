## Start simple, grow as you need

You can use the inbox for straightforward texting and calling right away. When you're ready, explore features like:

- **Group messages**: Send to multiple people at once
- **Start workflow**: For Wealthbox integrations, can kick off workflows from your inbox
- **Create task**: For some CRM integrations, can create a CRM task right from your inbox
- **Set reminders**: Don't forget to followup with your people by settings a reminder
- **Schedule messages**: Write the message now, but send it later

The inbox grows with you - use what you need today, discover more when you're ready.
