## Bulk Contact Operations

<Accordion title="Bulk Editing">
  Select multiple contacts and apply changes to all at once:
  - Add or remove tags from multiple contacts
  - Update company information across contacts  
  - Change contact status or categories
  - Export selected contacts
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Mass Delete Operations">
  **Safety First:** 
  - Always export contacts before mass deletion
  - Use filters to identify contacts to delete
  - Consider archiving instead of deleting  
  - Review deletion criteria carefully
</Accordion>

## Data Quality Maintenance

<Info>
  **Data Hygiene:** Regular contact maintenance improves deliverability rates and ensures more effective communication campaigns.
</Info>

### Common Maintenance Tasks

- **Phone Number Verification** - Check for disconnected numbers
- **Email Validation** - Remove bounced email addresses  
- **Duplicate Removal** - Merge or remove duplicate contacts
- **Tag Cleanup** - Remove unused or outdated tags
- **Contact Status Updates** - Update prospect to client status

### Contact Health Indicators

- **Complete Information** - All relevant fields populated
- **Recent Activity** - Regular interactions logged
- **Valid Contact Methods** - Working phone/email addresses
- **Proper Categorization** - Appropriate tags and segments assigned

<Tip>
  **Automation Tip:** Set up automated reminders to review and update contact information regularly. Most successful users review their contact database monthly.
</Tip>

## Contact Privacy & Compliance

<Warning>
  **Compliance Requirements:** Ensure your contact management practices comply with GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and other applicable privacy regulations.
</Warning>

Key compliance considerations:
- Obtain proper consent for communication
- Provide easy opt-out mechanisms  
- Honor do-not-contact requests immediately
- Secure contact data with appropriate access controls
- Document consent and communication preferences
