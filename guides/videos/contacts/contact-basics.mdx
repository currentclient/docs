## Upload Multiple Contacts

<iframe 
  src="https://www.loom.com/embed/6badf3c8546c41c3995e686fd9d0b849" 
  width="100%" 
  height={400}
  frameBorder={0} 
  allowFullScreen
/>

**Duration:** 8 minutes

Import multiple contacts at once using CSV files or other data sources. Perfect for migrating from another system or adding large contact lists.

<Info>
  **Supported Formats:** CSV, Excel files, and direct copy-paste from spreadsheets are all supported for bulk contact uploads.
</Info>

<Accordion title="Upload Requirements">
  - Phone numbers should be in E.164 format (+1234567890)
  - Email addresses must be valid format
  - Names should be separated into first and last name columns
  - Remove any duplicate entries before uploading
</Accordion>
