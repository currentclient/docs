## Message Scheduling Strategy

<Accordion title="Optimal Send Times">
  **Best practices for message timing:**
  - **Business Hours:** 9 AM - 5 PM in recipient's time zone
  - **Weekdays:** Tuesday through Thursday typically perform best
  - **Avoid:** Late evenings, early mornings, and weekends
  - **Special Events:** Consider holidays and industry-specific busy periods
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Time Zone Considerations">
  - Always schedule based on the recipient's local time
  - Use CurrentClient's automatic time zone detection
  - Double-check time zones for important messages
  - Consider daylight saving time changes
</Accordion>

<Tip>
  **Best Practice:** Schedule messages during business hours in your contact's time zone for better response rates.
</Tip>

## Conversation States

<Accordion title="Active vs Completed Conversations">
  **Active Conversations:**
  - Ongoing client discussions requiring follow-up
  - New messages or recent activity
  - Pending responses or actions needed
  
  **Completed Conversations:**  
  - Resolved client issues or completed transactions
  - Archived for record-keeping
  - No immediate action required
</Accordion>

## Inbox Efficiency Tips

### Quick Actions
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** - Learn hotkeys for common actions
- **Bulk Operations** - Select multiple conversations for mass actions
- **Search & Filter** - Find specific conversations quickly
- **Contact Context** - View full contact details without leaving inbox

### Response Templates
Create templates for:
- Initial responses and acknowledgments
- Common questions and answers
- Follow-up reminders
- Appointment confirmations
- Thank you messages

<Warning>
  **Response Time:** Quick response times improve client satisfaction and can significantly impact business outcomes. Aim to respond within 2-4 hours during business hours.
</Warning>
