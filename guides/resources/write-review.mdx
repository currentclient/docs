## What to include in your review

Not sure what to write? Here are some ideas to get you started:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share your experience">
    How has CurrentClient improved your client communication? What specific features do you find most valuable?
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mention your use case">
    Are you using it for client calls, text messaging, or both? What type of financial services do you provide?
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Highlight what you love">
    What's your favorite feature? Is it the compliance features, ease of use, or something else?
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Be different">
    Reviews can be a bit stuffy... so write soemthing punchy. Bonues points if you make us laugh.
  </Step>
</Steps>
