# Your quick start steps

Here's what we'll walk you through to get CurrentClient up and running. It's pretty straightforward and we'll be right here with you:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Signup for a free trial">
    [Create your CurrentClient account](/guides/getting-started/signup) and
    start your free trial - no commitment required. Explore all the features at
    your own pace.
  </Step>

<Step title="Install the apps">
  [Download and install the CurrentClient
  apps](/guides/getting-started/install-apps) on your desktop and mobile devices
  so you can stay connected anywhere.
</Step>

<Step title="Register your firm">
  [Register your firm](/guides/getting-started/register-firm) to ensure
  compliance with the latest phone carrier regulations.
</Step>

  <Step title="Port your number (optional)">
    [Transfer your existing phone number](/guides/porting/overview) to
    CurrentClient if you'd like. Your clients can reach you using the same
    number they know.
  </Step>
</Steps>
