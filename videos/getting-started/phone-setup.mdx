## Registration Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Your Business">
    Provide your business name, address, and tax ID information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Documentation">
    Upload required business verification documents
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phone Number Assignment">
    Select your business phone number from available options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compliance Verification">
    Complete TCPA compliance and consent verification
  </Step>
  <Step title="Testing">
    Test your phone setup with a verification call
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Registration Timeline

<Info>
  **Processing Time:** Phone registration typically takes 1-3 business days to complete. You can start using other CurrentClient features immediately while registration is pending.
</Info>

<Accordion title="What happens during registration review?">
  Carriers review your business information and use case to ensure compliance with telecommunications regulations. This process helps maintain high deliverability rates and prevents spam.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use CurrentClient before registration is complete?">
  Yes, you can access most CurrentClient features immediately. However, calling and SMS features will be limited until phone registration is approved.
</Accordion>

## After Registration

Once your phone registration is approved:

- ✅ Make unlimited business calls
- ✅ Send SMS broadcasts to your contacts  
- ✅ Receive incoming calls on your CurrentClient number
- ✅ Access advanced calling features like call recording
- ✅ Use the mobile app for calls on the go

<Tip>
  **Pro Tip:** Complete your phone registration as soon as possible after account creation to avoid any delays in accessing full communication features.
</Tip>
