## Advanced Batch Broadcasting

  src="https://www.loom.com/embed/78c1a175b80e4f7597b4779a239d7b5a" 
  width="100%" 
  height={400}
  frameBorder={0} 
  allowFullScreen
/>

**Duration:** 6 minutes

Learn advanced broadcasting techniques including batch sending to improve deliverability rates and avoid carrier restrictions.

<Accordion title="Why Use Batch Broadcasting?">
  - **Better Deliverability:** Carriers prefer gradual sending over bulk sends
  - **Improved Engagement:** Staggered sends can lead to better response rates  
  - **Compliance:** Helps maintain good standing with SMS carriers
  - **Analytics:** Better tracking of message performance over time
</Accordion>

<Warning>
  **Compliance Note:** Always ensure you have proper consent before sending broadcast messages. Follow TCPA guidelines and include opt-out options.
</Warning>
